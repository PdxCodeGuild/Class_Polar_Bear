Norm Macdonald, beloved comedian, died today at the age of 61

After a 9-year battle with cancer, it was reported early today September 14 that Norm Macdonald had passed away.

Norm Macdonald was a celebrated talk show host, and had been on Saturday Night Live in the mid-1990's
where he served as Weekend Update anchor as well as participated in many memorable skits.



“Comedy is surprises, so if you’re intending to make somebody laugh and they don’t laugh, that’s funny.” - Norm Macdonald
